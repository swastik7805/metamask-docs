### Configure for wallet aggregator only

To use Embedded Wallets (Web3Auth) as a wallet aggregator only (without social sign-in), disable social connections in your Web3Auth dashboard project settings.

Navigate to **Authentication > Social Connections** and turn off all social sign-in providers.
